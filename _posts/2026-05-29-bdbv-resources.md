ARTIC network has published a Bundibugyo virus (BDBV) sequencing guide as the 2026 outbreak continues in the DRC and Uganda. We have compiled this for labs wishing to sequence, assemble and analyse Bundibugyo virus genomes: [artic.network/viruses/bdbv/bdbv-sequencing-guide.html](https://artic.network/viruses/bdbv/bdbv-sequencing-guide.html).

The guide covers sequencing approaches involving amplicon panels, bait capture, and metagenomics for Bundibugyo and Ebolavirus surveillance. A new BDBV-specific primer scheme ([`artic-bdbv-2026/400/v1.0.0`](https://labs.primalscheme.com/detail/artic-bdbv-2026/400/v1.0.0/?q=bdbv)) has been published and is currently undergoing testing with partners. An update to the existing pan-Ebola scheme will be released imminently. Guidance for assembling and analysing BDBV sequences is included, along with advice on sharing data through [Pathoplexus](https://pathoplexus.org/), which now supports Bundibugyo virus submissions.
